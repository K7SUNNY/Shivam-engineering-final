Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common technical queries about our pipeline solutions and compliance standards

What safety standards do you follow for gas pipeline construction?

We execute all pipeline laying projects in strict accordance with the **ASME B31.8** standard for Gas Transmission and Distribution Piping Systems, as well as the safety regulations established by the **Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB)** of India. All welding operators are certified under **API 1104** regulations.

Do you handle regulatory approvals and environmental clearances?

Yes. As a part of our turnkey execution services, we draft route plans and compile engineering designs for local authorities (NHAI, Forest Department, municipal bodies) to secure necessary Right of Use (RoU) clearances and statutory permissions.

What materials do you specialize in for industrial pipeline laying?

We specialize in laying high-strength Carbon Steel pipelines (API 5L grade) for high-pressure gas distribution, Stainless Steel for chemical processes, and High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) lines for utility water networks and low-pressure gas distribution systems.

How do you perform quality and weld integrity testing?

We perform 100% Non-Destructive Testing (NDT), including radiographic weld examination, ultrasonic inspection, and hydrostatic pressure testing at 1.5 times the design pressure limit. This guarantees leak-free pipeline configurations prior to final gas-in or commissioning.
