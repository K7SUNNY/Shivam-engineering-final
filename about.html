About Shivam Engineering

Engineering excellence since 2019, delivering world-class pipeline solutions with innovation, integrity, and unwavering commitment to quality.

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by visionary engineer Satyavir Singh, Shivam Engineering emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize pipeline infrastructure development across India and beyond.

What started as a small engineering consultancy has grown into a leading pipeline solutions provider, serving major industrial clients and government projects. Our commitment to innovation, safety, and environmental responsibility has earned us recognition as industry pioneers.

Today, with over 15 successful projects and a team of 50+ expert engineers, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in pipeline engineering and infrastructure development.
