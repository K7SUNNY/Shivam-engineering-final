About Shivam Engineering

Engineering excellence since 2019, delivering world-class pipeline solutions with innovation, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to safety.

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by visionary engineer Satyavir Singh, Shivam Engineering emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize pipeline infrastructure development across India through superior engineering standards.

What started as a small consulting group has grown into a leading industrial pipeline solutions provider, delivering infrastructure networks for major energy clients including GAIL Gas Limited and Indraprastha Gas Limited (IGL).

Today, with over 25 successful high-pressure steel projects completed and an active team of 50+ expert engineers, we continue to set benchmarks in pipeline safety, Cathodic protection installations, and urban utility distribution.
